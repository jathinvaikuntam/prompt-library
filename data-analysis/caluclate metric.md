# calculate metric

## Purpose: 

To calculate a metric while explaining the logic.

## Prompt: 

Calculate the Year-over-Year (YoY) growth rate. Show your work step-by-step.

# Example Input: 

"Last year revenue: 500,000. Current year revenue: 650,000."

# Example Output:

1.Find the difference: 650,000 - 500,000 = 150,000.
2.Divide the difference by the original value: 150,000 / 500,000 = 0.3.
3.Multiply by 100 to get the percentage: 0.3 * 100 = 30%.
4.The YoY growth rate is 30%.
