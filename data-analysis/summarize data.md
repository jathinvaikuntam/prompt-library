# summarize data

 ## Purpose
 
 To summarize the key findings of a dataset description.

## Prompt 

Summarize the main trends in this sales data summary.

# Example Input: 

"Q1 Sales: 10k, Q2 Sales: 12k, Q3 Sales: 8k (Major supply chain disruption), Q4 Sales: 15k."

# Example Output: 

Sales showed steady growth in the first half of the year, suffered a significant dip in Q3 due to supply issues, but recovered strongly in Q4 to reach a yearly high
