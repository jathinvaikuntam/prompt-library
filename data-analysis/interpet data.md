# interpeting data

## Purpose:

 To interpret data for a non-technical audience.
## Prompt:

 You are a Data Scientist. Explain the concept of "Correlation vs. Causation" to a marketing manager using a retail example.

# Example Input:

 "Ice cream sales and sunscreen sales both go up in July."

# Example Output: 

"While both sales rise together (correlation), buying ice cream doesn't cause someone to buy sunscreen. Instead, the hot weather is the 'hidden variable' causing both. In marketing, we must ensure our campaigns target the actual cause of a sale, not just a related trend."
