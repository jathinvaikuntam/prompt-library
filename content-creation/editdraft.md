# edit draft

## Purpose

To edit a draft from the perspective of a ruthless professional editor.

## Prompt

You are a world-class magazine editor. Rewrite the following paragraph to be more concise, punchy, and engaging.

# Example Input

There are many people who think that AI is going to change the world in a big way, but some people are scared of it."

# Example Output

 While AI promises a global metamorphosis, a deep-seated anxiety shadows its rise. Is it a tool for progress, or a catalyst for chaos?
